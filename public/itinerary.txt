
Itinerary Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2025

Echo Labs AG ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information in connection with the Itinerary application (the "App").

What Information Does Itinerary Collect?

Itinerary is a calendar utility and does not collect or store any personal information from your device. This includes, but is not limited to:

Photos or videos
Location data
Contacts
Device identifiers
Usage data
How Does Itinerary Use Information?

Itinerary does not use any information collected from your device. The App functions solely to allow you to display your calendar events on a map for your own use.

Does Itinerary Share Information with Third Parties?

Itinerary does not share any information with third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access to or disclosure of information through Itinerary. However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is completely secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Choices

Because Itinerary does not collect any information, there are no specific options to control your information. You can uninstall the App at any time if you no longer wish to use it.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

 We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
